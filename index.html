BIOGRAPHY!

-Bachelor of Arts in Entrepreneurial Management
-Working on Masters in Data Analytics

California State University San Bernardino graduate with a Bachelors of Arts in Entrepreneurial Management (June of 2018). The courses provided me with knowledge about formulating innovative and distinctive ideas. I was taught about fundamental business models and how to minimize risks in order to become a successful entrepreneur.

After graduating I started working at Yale Elementary. I noticed that most of the teachers were recording information such as students’ attendance, log-in attempts, attitudes, and attention spans in order to generate grade scores at the end of each semester. This is when I started getting passionate about the field of data analytics because I saw how the teachers used the data to monitor and improve student development. This made me realize how crucial it is to utilize data and information not only for individual students’ but how companies can use it for business growth and development as well.

This drove me to pursue my masters degree in Data Analytics at NorthEastern University. I am continually working on new projects that improve my data analytics skills along with my programming skills using languages such as Python, R studio, and SQL. With these skills I hope to find a fulfilling career at a company and help them reach their goals.
